About removals in Runcorn

A Halton town connected to Widnes by the iconic Silver Jubilee Bridge, with a large new town area and good motorway links. Runcorn is a significant removal market on the southern edge of Merseyside, with a large population across both the old town and new town areas built in the 1960s and 70s. Its easy access to the M56 makes it attractive for commuters working across Cheshire and Greater Manchester, and the town sees steady removal demand from both its social housing stock and growing private sector.

Local knowledge matters: The Silver Jubilee Bridge connecting Runcorn to Widnes has height and weight restrictions for some large vehicles — confirm your driver's vehicle specifications before booking for cross-river moves.
